# Tax Calculator Scope

## Product Lines

Crypto Audit Report is evolving into three related product lines:

1. Crypto Tax Calculator / Tax-Readiness
2. Bank / Source-of-Funds Audit Package
3. Portfolio Analytics

These product lines can share imported transaction history, deterministic
classification, review findings, report preparation, and document checklists.
They must not blur compliance boundaries or make official claims that the
product cannot support.

## Planned Tax Calculator Scope

The tax calculator is planned product scope. It should help users prepare a
preliminary, deterministic tax-readiness estimate from user-provided crypto
transaction data.

Safe product claim:

> Crypto Audit Report helps users prepare a preliminary, deterministic
> tax-readiness estimate from user-provided crypto transaction data.

The current product is not official tax filing. The MVP must not process tax
payments, submit declarations, or guarantee tax correctness.

## Current MVP Boundaries

The current MVP may prepare transaction history, review findings,
source-of-funds explanations, report previews, document checklists, local
exports, and tax-readiness documentation.

The current MVP must not claim to provide:

- official tax due;
- official tax filing;
- tax payment processing;
- legal advice;
- tax advice;
- financial advice;
- AML certification;
- bank approval.

## Requirements For Future Tax Modules

Every tax module must be deterministic and reproducible. Every future tax
module must include:

- methodology;
- jurisdiction scope;
- supported operation types;
- unsupported operation types;
- tests;
- disclaimers;
- excluded-from-estimate behavior.

Tax modules must make unsupported or incomplete cases visible. No operation
should silently disappear from the user-facing result.

## Unsafe Product Claims

Do not use these claims in product UI, reports, documentation, marketing, or PR
descriptions:

- "official tax due"
- "guaranteed tax correctness"
- "file your taxes automatically"
- "pay tax inside the app"
- "avoid tax"
- "tax optimization"
- "legal proof"
- "AML clean"
- "bank approved"

## Safe Language

Use wording that keeps the product in tax-readiness and review preparation:

- preliminary estimate;
- draft calculation;
- deterministic estimate;
- tax-readiness;
- for accountant/tax-consultant review;
- needs review;
- excluded from estimate;
- unsupported in this version;
- based on user-provided data.
